Driving

Driving from Edinburgh takes us a wee bit less than 4 hours. Depending on your driving route it's very roughly £75 for fuel (based on 35mpg and taking the A9 in around 3 hours 45 mins).

Public transport

Public transport takes up to around 7 hours - it's a twenty second walk from the train station so public transport is a great option!

By train, the Edininburgh/Perth/Inverness/Achnasheen (2 changes) route takes 5 and a half hours or the Edinburgh/Inverness/Achnasheen (1 change) route takes around 6 and a half hours. Costs are variable but a mid-2019 rough calculation of 1 week in advance for 2 adults and 1 child was around £140 total for the return journey.

It's also possible to travel by bus in around 6 and a half hours using the Megabus M90 from Edinbrugh to Inverness, then Service 700 (Laide) by Westerbus. Cost estimate for 2 adults and 1 child was around £165 for the return journey.

How much does it cost?

It's free! Genuinely free. Honestly free. We want our friends and family to enjoy it and for it to be as occupied as much as possible throughout the year. So please, we'd love you to book in and head up there.

When can I go?

You can go whenever it's free! Or, if you don't mind sharing, maybe even when it's already booked! Find a date in the calendar and drop Jono & Ruth an email to book.

What can I do in the area?

Once we've explored the area a bit more we'll have a list of places to dine and things to do - in the meantime let us know if you've got any hot tips!

  • Hillwalking - can't wait to try the Muriusg-Carn Gorm tops, or to try the long but easy valley walk that Ruth found
  • Geocaching
  • Stop at the Contin Village Store for an ice cream
  • The Midge Bite (the old Achnasheen filling station) for a cuppa and a cake (2019-08-24: was closed)
  • Ledgowan Lodge Hotel for a cuppa
  • Gairloch (40 minutes drive)
  • Ullapool (1 hour drive)
  • Inverness (45 minutes drive)
  • Torridon (30 minutes drive) (you could have a fancy dinner at The Torridon)
  • Applecross (70 minutes drive)
  • Kyle of Lochalsh and Skye Bridge (1 hour drive)
  • Dingwall (40 minutes drive)
  • Dornoch (80 minutes drive)
